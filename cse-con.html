B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering

B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering programme seeks to produce graduates who excel in computer science and engineering education with international standards for global employment and research. The programme focuses on equipping graduates with the skills to make their mark in the rapidly changing world of technology and build a successful career in the field of software engineering. A B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering teaches students to design and develop computer-based applications of varying complexities in emerging areas of Computer Science and Engineering. The programme delivers specific capabilities for becoming experts in the field of computer engineering. A degree in this program will cover the following topics:

* Fundamentals of engineering

* Computation and algorithms

* Programming languages

* Program and Application Design

* Hardware, Software, Networking & Security
