B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering ( Cyber Security )

B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) programme seeks to produce competent Cyber Security professionals who have the right theoretical and practical knowledge to secure software and hardware systems. The programme teaches students about the ethical security practices to create a secure cyberspace and develop sustainable solutions that satisfy the needs of the modern industry. A B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) will enable students to become professionals who can secure the technical world from all kinds of threats. Through this programme, students will acquire theoretical and practical knowledge and insights into cybersecurity. They will also develop a good understanding of the industry practices and analytical methods in cybersecurity. A degree in this program will cover the following topics:

* Cyber Crime & Network Security

* Data Structure

* Design and Analysis of Algorithms

* Cryptography

* Intrusion Detection and Prevention
